La Union, Philippines

The Philippines is a Southeast Asian archipelago of over 7,100 islands in the western Pacific Ocean, known for its diverse geography, including rainforests, mountains, volcanoes, and beaches. Situated on the Pacific Ring of Fire, the country experiences frequent seismic and volcanic activity. Its main island groups are Luzon in the north, the Visayas in the center, and Mindanao in the south. Historically a Spanish colony and later a U.S. territory, the Philippines has a rich, complex history and a culture influenced by both traditions.
