AC and DC Machinery Gallery

Site Name

AC and DC Machinery Gallery - This website is a gallery of different types of AC and DC machines. It includes images and descriptions of various machines used in electrical engineering.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a visual reference and educational resource for students and professionals in the field of electrical engineering. It aims to showcase the different types of AC and DC machines and their applications.

Color Schema

The color scheme for the website will be a combination of blue #004080, white #FFFFFF, and gray #f9f9f9.

Blue will be used for the header and footer, white for the background, and gray for the text and other elements. This color scheme is chosen to create a professional and clean look that is easy on the eyes.

Typography

The typography for the website will be Golos Text with different font weight, for h1 - 900; h2 - 600; and p - 400.
